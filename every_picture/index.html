Melt — Not For Radio

I got this CD a few days after I went to the Not For Radio concert in San Francisco. It was a magical night filled with incredible music and unforgettable memories. The energy in the venue was ethereal, heartbreaking, and felt so magical. The theme was set around Swan Lake, and as the first concert of 2026, it was a good way to start off my concert-going experience.
The song that plays is Swan. The song explores vulnerability, heartbreak, and moving past heartbreak all in an embodiment of a Swan, symbolizing eternal love and devotion. It's my favorite song from her album.

Every track feels attached to a memory, which makes this one of the most meaningful albums in my collection.
